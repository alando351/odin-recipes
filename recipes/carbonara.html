Carbonara

Carbonara is a wonderful creamy blend of pasta, bacon, black pepper and parmigiano cheese.

Ingredients

  • 1 box of dry pasta
  • 4oz of bacon
  • 2 Large Eggs
  • 1 cup of grated Parigiao Reggiano cheese
  • Fresh Ground Black Pepper

Directions

  1. Bring a pot of salted water to a boil and add the pasta. Cook for 8 to 10 minutes until al dente. Drain the pasta while reserving a cup of the starchy water.
  2. Dice the bacon into small strips and add it to a cold pan turned onto medium heat. Cook until the bacon is crisp and the fat is rendered. Drain the fat from the bacon and let it rest.
  3. Toss the spaghetti with the bacon and some of the bacon fat. In a mixing bowl beat the eggs and cheese together. Once they're fully incorporated add in the pasta bacon mixture and continue mixing until the sauce has thickened and is homogenus. If neceessary thin out the sauce with the remaining pasta water. To serve add in the fresh ground black pepper and some extra grated cheese.
