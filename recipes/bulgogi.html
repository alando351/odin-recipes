Bulgogi

Bulgogi is a korean bbq beef dish. Consisting of thinly sliced marinated beef cooked to tender perfection.

Ingredients

  • 1.5lbs of ribeye steak sliced thinly
  • half a small pear or apple peeled and grated
  • 1/4th cup of soy sauce
  • 2 tablespoons of brown sugar
  • 2 tablesppons of sesame oil
  • 3 cloves of garlic minced
  • 1 tablesppon of gochujang (korean red pepper paste)
  • 2 green onions thinly sliced
  • 1 teaspoon of toasted sesame seeds

Directions

  1. Prepare steak and vegetables slicing as directed in ingredients list.
  2. In a medium bowl combine pear, soy sauce, brown sugar, sesame oil, garlic and gochujang. Combine marinaed and sliced steak in a zip log bag marinating for at least 2 hours and upto overnight.
  3. In a cast iron pan add 1 tablespoon of neutral oil and in batches add the steak to the pan in a single layer flipping on occasion until there is a nice charr and it has been cooked to roughly 2-3 minutes per side.
  4. Serve immediateley garnishing it with green onions and sesame seeds. This dish is best eaten with rice!
