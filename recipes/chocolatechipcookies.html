Chocolate Chip Cookies

This recipe is for delicious chocolate chip cookies utilizing browned butter and is by Bon Appetit.

Ingredients

  • 1.5 Cups of Flour
  • 1 tsp of Salt
  • 3/4th tsp of baking soda
  • 3/4 cup of unsalted butter
  • 1 cup of brown sugar
  • 1/4th cup of regular sugar
  • 1 large egg
  • 1 egg yolk
  • 2 tsp of vanilla extract
  • 6oz of bittersweete chocolate
  • Optional 2.5oz of pecans

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 375 degrees F. Whisk flour, salt and baking soda together in a bowl.
  2. Heat half of the buttere in a saucepain over medium until it starts to brown and smell nutty. Transfer to a bowl and let it cool once it is a deep brown.
  3. Whisk together butter and sugar until smooth, add egg, yolk and vanilla extract until completley incorporated together. Fold in dry ingredients with the butter mixture until smooth. Lastly fold in chocolate and let the dough rest 5-10 minutes
  4. Using an ince cream scoop portion the dough into roughly 16 balls and place them 2 inches apart on a parchment lined baking sheet. Bake in the oven for 8-10 minutes until deeply golden brown.
